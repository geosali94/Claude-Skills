# linkedin-audit

**Pipeline position:** Step 1 of 2 — LinkedIn Pipeline

## What it does

Benchmarks your existing LinkedIn profile against strong public guidance and best practices. It reads your current profile alongside your resume and any available GitHub activity, then identifies weak positioning, missing metrics, under-utilized sections, and cross-source gaps.

## Inputs

- Your current LinkedIn profile (paste as text or provide a PDF export)
- Resume (for cross-referencing experience and metrics)
- (Optional) GitHub profile URL or project list

## Output

A **LinkedIn Audit Summary** containing:
- Headline and About section assessment
- Experience entry quality scores
- Missing or weak sections identified
- Cross-source gaps (things on your resume not reflected on LinkedIn)
- Grouped clarifying questions to gather missing information

## Next step

Feed the Audit Summary into [`linkedin-formatter`](../linkedin-formatter/) to produce the full rewritten profile.
