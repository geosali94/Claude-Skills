# jd-resume-auditor

**Pipeline position:** Step 1 of 4 — Resume & Cover Letter Pipeline

## What it does

Compares your current resume against a target job description and produces a structured gap report. It identifies missing keywords, under-represented skills, and positioning mismatches — giving you a clear picture of what needs to change before rewriting.

## Inputs

- Your current resume (any format — paste as text or provide as a file)
- The target job description (paste in full)

## Output

A structured **Gap Report** containing:
- Matched qualifications (what already lands)
- Missing keywords and skills
- Positioning gaps (where your framing doesn't align with the role)
- Prioritized list of items to address in the next step

## Next step

Feed the Gap Report into [`context-gatherer`](../context-gatherer/) to collect the additional information needed to close the identified gaps.
